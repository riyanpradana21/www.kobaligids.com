Bali Barat National Park

Description : West Bali National Park is the location for the release of Bali starlings. The Bali starling is the rarest bird species in the world. There are no more than four of these dandy birds in nature. Several other potential natural resources that are of interest for research and recreation include the bull animal (Bos Javanicus), pure natural forest of Sawo kecik (Manilkara kauki), mangrove forest, marine biota, natural hot springs, natural panorama of forests and beaches and cultural heritage objects. Its location in the center of a tourist destination (Bali Island), and supported by well-managed facilities for accommodation, transformation and cultural tourism attractions, makes this national park very interesting to visit.

South Bali Tours

Garuda Wisnu Kencana Statue

Description : The Garuda Wisnu Kencana statue is located on Unggasan Hill - Jimbaran, Bali. This statue stands towering in the Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park complex and is the work of the famous Balinese sculptor, I Nyoman Nuarta. This monument was developed as a cultural park and has become an icon for Bali and Indonesian tourism. The statue is in the form of God Vishnu, who in Hinduism is the Sustainer God (Sthiti), riding the Garuda bird. The character of Garuda can be seen in the story of Garuda & his Kingdom which tells the story of the Garuda bird's devotion and sacrifice to save his mother from slavery who was ultimately protected by the God Vishnu. This statue is projected to bind the spatial layout with a visibility distance of up to 20 km so that it can be seen from Kuta, Sanur, Nusa Dua to Tanah Lot. The Garuda Wisnu Kencana statue is a symbol of the mission to save the environment and the world.
