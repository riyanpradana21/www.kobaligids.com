About Us

Let me first introduce myself to you: My name is Nyoman Suwastika, my nickname is Komang or Ko. I was born in Bali, that's why my name is ¨Komang¨. For us, the name means the third birth in the family or the third child. I was born on December 9 in a small village in western Bali. I am now married and living in Denpasar City, Bali with my Wife and two children. I lived in the Netherlands with my family for a year and learned Dutch there. After I returned to Bali from the Netherlands, I became a Dutch-speaking guide. I enjoy my work as a guide. Not only because I can earn rupiah with it, but also because I can always use my knowledge of Dutch and show my (our) beautiful island as a tourist attraction. Bali is not alone: ​​The holiday resorts of Kuta, Sanur or Nusa Dua. There are many more beautiful places on this island where few or no tourists come.
